How to play

Goal:

Guess the RGB values of the color on the left.

Move the sliders to input values. Hit guess when you think you've got it.

Scoring:

Your score each round is the average distance from the RGB values you guessed to the correct ones. The lower your score, the closer you were. 0 is a perfect score.

After 5 rounds, round scores will be added and your final score given.

Make your first guess to get going!
